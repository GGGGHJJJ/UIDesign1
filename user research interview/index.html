Topic

: Website for finding restaurants Each restaurant's reviews and expert evaluations are included to recommend restaurants that you can trust and visit. This is a website that automatically recommends you if you enter your location, food menu, and personal rating (e.g. spicy, etc.). The website is a bit of a game format, and if you enter a keyword into the game area, it recommends the corresponding restaurant. (The game is considering things like a wheel, fortune cookies, and lottery.) -> The website is structured in the form of a slot machine. When you write down your location, food menu, and personal rating, and press the button on the machine, a page recommending restaurants appears.
